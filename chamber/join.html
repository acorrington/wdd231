Become a Member of the Roseburg Chamber of Commerce

Join the Roseburg Chamber of Commerce to connect with local businesses, access resources, and grow your organization. Fill out the membership application form below to get started.

Membership Application

Thank you for your interest in joining the Roseburg Chamber of Commerce! Please fill out the application form below.

Choose the membership level that best suits your organization:

Non Profit

Perfect for non-profit organizations looking to connect and grow.

Bronze

Entry-level membership for small businesses and startups.

Silver

Enhanced benefits for growing businesses seeking more exposure.

Gold

Premium membership with maximum benefits and visibility.

Non Profit Membership Benefits

  • $0 annual membership fee
  • Access to networking events
  • Training workshops
  • Listing in the Chamber directory

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • $500 annual membership fee
  • All Non Profit benefits
  • Business spotlight in newsletter (1x/year)
  • Event discounts
  • Ribbon cutting ceremony

Silver Membership Benefits

  • $1000 annual membership fee
  • All Bronze benefits
  • Featured listing on home page
  • Priority event registration
  • Free training sessions

Gold Membership Benefits

  • $1500 annual membership fee
  • All Silver benefits
  • Premium advertising placement
  • Exclusive networking events
  • Personalized business consulting
